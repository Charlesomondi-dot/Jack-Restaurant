Our Story

Fire, flavor, and genuine hospitality.

Jack's Hearth Kitchen is a chef-led restaurant celebrating wood fire, coastal brightness, and Hill Country ingredients. Our open hearth anchors the dining room while our team shares dishes meant for lingering evenings and lively gatherings.

We partner with nearby ranchers and farms, bake our sourdough daily, and balance every plate with freshness and texture. Whether it's date night, a business dinner, or a family celebration, we make it effortless and warm.
